About Us

  2. Who we are

Ascension Primary school is a Catholic school found in Blessed Trinity Nalweyo Parish Diocese of Hoima Uganda.

The Motto of the School is .

" To honor God and dignity for all"

The school is located on the beautiful hill of kijwenge in Kakumiro District.
It serves children from different background especially the orphans. The school is connected to Our Lady of Mercy Children's home also a ministry of Blessed Trinity.

As a rural school, we hold the following values dear to our hearts. These values guide us in all our actions and decisions and define who we are as a community

  • Community - we strive to create a welcoming an dinclusive environment.
  • Faith- Our catholic faith is at the heart of everything we do
  • Service- We are called to serve others
  • Excellence- We strive for excellence in all aspects of our school life

We are proud to uphold these values whic are rooted in our faith and inspire us to be the best version of our selves
