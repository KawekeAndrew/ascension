About Us

Ascension Primary school is a Catholic school found in Blessed Trinity Nalweyo Parish Diocese of Hoima Uganda.
The school is located on the beautiful hill of kijwenge in Kakumiro District. It serves children from different background especially the orphans. The school is connected to Our Lady of Mercy Children's home also a ministry of Blessed Trinity.
